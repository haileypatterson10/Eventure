Accessibility Report

Ensuring our app works for everyone

👁️

Visual Accessibility

High contrast ratios, screen reader compatible, resizable text, and meaningful alt text for images.

⌨️

Keyboard Navigation

Full keyboard accessibility, visible focus states, and logical tab order throughout the application.

🔊

Audio Alternatives

Captions for video content, transcripts for audio, and visual notifications for audio alerts.

📱

Device Compatibility

Responsive design that works across all device sizes and orientations, from mobile to desktop.
